SPLIT
About map:

Split is a vertical, urban-themed map in Valorant known for its tight choke points, elevated positions, and heavily defender-favored design due to narrow pathways and limited entry options.

Split is set in a Japanese-inspired metropolis, likely in a region similar to Tokyo. The map’s aesthetic blends traditional cityscapes with futuristic advancements.

Controlling Mid is crucial for rotations and splitting the defense. The ziplines (ropes) provide vertical movement, making it essential to watch for flanks and surprise plays.

BIND
About map:

Desert-themed with sandy textures and Middle Eastern-inspired architecture.

There are unique one-way teleporters that enable fast rotations but produce a loud sound, alerting enemies.

Unlike many other maps, Bind has no traditional "mid" lane, making site control and flanking routes crucial.

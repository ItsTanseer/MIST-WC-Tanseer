HAVEN
About map:

Haven is one of the most unique maps in Valorant, known for its three bomb sites—A, B, and C—making it stand out from other maps that typically have only two

The map is set in a monastery in Bhutan, blending traditional architecture with modern elements.

Features destructible panels at Mid, which can be broken to create new sightlines and opportunities.
